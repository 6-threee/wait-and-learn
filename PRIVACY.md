# Privacy Policy — Agora

Last updated: 2026-06-13

Agora is a free, local tool. It does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal
data, and it has no account, server, or sign-in.

## What Agora stores

Your spaced-repetition progress and settings are stored locally on your device
only:

- **Chrome extension:** in the browser's `chrome.storage.local`.
- **Claude Code plugin:** in `~/.agora/` on your machine.

This data never leaves your device.

## What Agora sends

Nothing. Agora makes no network requests. It has no analytics, no telemetry, and
no third-party trackers.

- The Chrome extension runs only on `https://claude.ai/*`. It inspects the page
  solely to detect when Claude is generating a response, and never collects,
  stores, or transmits your conversations or any page content.
- Pronunciation uses your device's built-in text-to-speech voices. No audio is
  sent anywhere.

## Permissions (Chrome extension)

- `storage` — to save your progress locally.
- Host access to `https://claude.ai/*` — to show the flashcard while Claude
  thinks.

## Payments

Agora is free. An optional "pay what you want" tip is handled entirely by
Gumroad; if you choose to tip, Gumroad's own privacy policy applies to that
transaction. Agora itself never receives any payment information.
